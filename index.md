## Getting Started

### How to Play

1. **Choose a Category**: Select from 13 different categories like Everyday Actions, Food & Cooking, or Emotions & Feelings
2. **Select a Level**: Pick a word level to start playing
3. **View the Image**: Look at the image displayed and try to guess the word
4. **Use Hints**: If you need help, you can use:
   - Sentence hint (shows the word in context)
   - Explanation hint (provides the meaning)
   - Reveal letter (costs 2 points from your final score)
5. **Earn Points**: Get points for correct answers and track your progress

### First Time Setup

- Complete the onboarding screens when you first open the app
- Browse through the available categories
- Select a category to begin your learning journey
- Start with free words (first 5 words in each category)

### Scoring System

- Base points: 10 points per correct answer
- Hints: -2 points per hint used
- Revealed letters: -2 points per letter revealed
- Minimum score: 0 points (you can't go negative)

## Troubleshooting

### App Crashes or Freezes

1. Force quit the app and restart it
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS
4. Reinstall the app if the problem persists (your progress is saved)

### Images Not Displaying

- Ensure images are properly loaded for the category
- Check your internet connection (if applicable)
- Try restarting the app

### Progress Not Saving

- Progress saves automatically after each completed word
- Make sure you're signed in to iCloud (if using iCloud sync)
- Don't delete the app without backing up your data

### Can't Reveal Letters

- Make sure the word is fully loaded
- You can reveal letters even with 0 points (costs are deducted from final score)

### Purchase Issues

- See the "In-App Purchases" section below
- Contact support with your Apple ID and purchase date

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q: How many words are in the app?

A: Free users get 5 words per category (65 total words). Premium unlocks 400+ additional words across all categories.

### Q: Can I use the app offline?

A: Yes! Once downloaded, the app works completely offline.

### Q: Will my progress sync across devices?

A: Currently, progress is stored locally on each device. We're working on cloud sync for future updates.

### Q: How do I reset my progress?

A: Tap the reset button (circular arrow icon) in the top-right corner of the Categories screen. Confirm to reset all progress and score.

### Q: Can I change the language?

A: Currently, WordVision is available in English. More languages coming soon!

### Q: Why can't I see all words in a category?

A: Free users see the first 5 words per category. Unlock premium to access all words.

### Q: How do I earn more points?

A: Answer correctly without using hints. Each hint reduces your final score by 2 points.

### Q: What happens if I delete the app?

A: All local progress will be lost. Premium purchases can be restored after reinstalling.
